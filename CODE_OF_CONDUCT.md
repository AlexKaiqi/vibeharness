# Code of Conduct

VibeHarness welcomes practical, respectful collaboration.

Expected behavior:

- discuss ideas and tradeoffs in good faith;
- credit prior work clearly;
- protect sensitive user, repository, customer, and episode data;
- keep feedback specific and actionable;
- avoid harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.

Unacceptable behavior may result in moderation, issue locking, or removal from
the project space.

Until formal governance exists, maintainers are responsible for interpreting and
enforcing this policy.
